Membership & FAQ

Joining the Covenant
How do I join?
There are 4 ways to join: Attend the National Conference or a Regional Gathering. If you are the first in your area, fill out the form below and we will help you start a Region.
Are there financial fees?
No fees to join, stay, or leave. We operate by Covenant. We may ask for a seed to help defray conference costs.
Do you affirm women in clergy?
YES! We fully affirm and support women in ministry leadership and clergy roles.
Is this a denomination?
No, we are an Alliance. We respect local church autonomy. Episcopal supervision is available upon request but never assumed.
Why "Chairman" instead of "Presiding Bishop"?
The next leader may not be a Bishop. The Founder intends to serve 4-6 years before passing the Chairmanship.

Our Mandate

Founded by Bishop J. D. Charlot, Sr., the Alliance seeks to re-establish the Move of God that began in Pontiac, MI in 2019. We are a body of believers dedicated to the apostles' doctrine, fellowship, and prayer. We are not just an organization; we are a Covenant family.

Common Questions

How do I join? +

There are 4 ways to join: Attend the National Conference or a Regional Gathering. If you are the first in your area, fill out the form below to start a Region.

Are there fees? +

No Fees to Join, No Fees to Stay, No Fees to Leave. We operate by Covenant. We may ask for a seed to help defray conference costs.

Women in Clergy? +

YES! We fully affirm and support women in ministry leadership and clergy roles.

Church Autonomy +

We are an Alliance, not a denomination. Episcopal Supervision may be requested, but never assumed.
